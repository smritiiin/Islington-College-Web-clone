Why Islington?


At islington, we will not only push you and make you work and study harder than you have ever before but also make you enjoy the process, every step of the way. Being an institution with a long history, Islington College has developed many leaders and entrepreneurs. It assures that every student is technically competent. Islington College is a modern and practical education based college dedicated to excellence.

What a Student develop's after studying here?


Here at islington, we aim to provide not only academic skills that are required but also some of the most important skills that is required in the modern world, which are team-work among a people with diverse skillset, presentation and public speaking skill, competitiveness and growth mindset.

Be a Part of Excellence

Teaching Methodology


We adopt a unique and internationally applied teaching approach, L-T-W (Lecture, Tutorial, and Workshop). L-T-W teaching approach equips students with practical experience derived from their learning in the classroom.

Excess Learning opportunities


We, here at islington, strongly believe that education should be a fun process, where creativeness and curiosity play a big impact on how a student learns. With this notion, we have many fun and exciting skill development programs throughout the year to keep our students engaged in learning and finding themselves. From sports to business plans, we have focused on all round development of the students.
